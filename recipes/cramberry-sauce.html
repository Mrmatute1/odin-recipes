cranberry sauce

this cranberry sauce was made with a lot of love

  • fresh cranberries
  • sugar
  • orange juice
  1. Heat orange juice in a saucepan
  2. Stir in sugar and cook until it dissolves.
  3. Add cranberries and continue cooking, stirring often, until they start to pop.
  4. Remove the sauce from heat and transfer it to a bowl
  5. the cranberry sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.
  6. enjoy
